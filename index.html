Every monetizable asset in the ecosystem — explained, packaged, and protected

You're accessing a 3000+ TLD protocol empire. Each namespace is an engine — and each engine has licensable components.

Every TLD (route) in our portfolio produces multiple revenue points. Here's how they break down:

🌐

TLD Licensing (Namespace Access)

Grant access to use the top-level domain (TLD) or create a wrapper brand/product on top of it.

💸 Monetization Methods:
  • White-label licensing for third-party use (e.g. textme.com builds on .textme)
  • Exclusive vertical rights (e.g. .signatureai licensed to luxury brands)
  • International mirror rights (e.g. .chamberofcommerce licensed to national registries)
🧾 Deliverables:
  • Signed namespace access agreement
  • Revenue share, flat license fee, or limited-term lease
  • Use restrictions + on-chain tracking
🪪

Subdomain Minting & Leasing

Sell, lease, or assign subdomains under any TLD to individuals, brands, or AI agents.

💸 Monetization Methods:
  • Flat-rate or auction-style subdomain sales (e.g. poet.sonnet, realtor.closingagent)
  • Subscription-based leasing (e.g. $5–$50/month for handle/identity access)
  • Premium tier minting (e.g. vanity, reserved, or legacy names)
🧾 Deliverables:
  • Smart contract + hosted agent environment
  • Mint license terms or lease agreements
  • Agent provisioning access

AI Agent Licensing

Deploy named, tone-matched AI agents under each protocol for customer service, real estate, poetry/writing, and commerce.

  • Custom agent development for enterprise clients
🧾 Deliverables:

Everything you need to know about the Syndicate Ledger

Is this a security or investment offering?

+

No. This is not a security, equity offering, or traditional investment. You are contributing to protocol development and receiving structured access rights, licensing benefits, and utility within the ecosystem. Think of it as a digital infrastructure license with yield mechanics.

What exactly am I buying?

+

You're purchasing access rights to protocol infrastructure, including subdomain minting privileges, AI agent licenses, profit participation (depending on tier), and priority access to future protocol launches. You're not buying ownership of the core assets.

How are profits calculated and distributed?

+

Profits are calculated from protocol monetization including subdomain sales, AI agent licensing, infrastructure access fees, and enterprise partnerships. Distributions are made quarterly via your preferred method (crypto, bank transfer, or reinvestment).

Can I upgrade my tier later?

+

Yes, tier upgrades are available based on availability. You pay the difference between your current tier and the new tier, and your benefits are upgraded immediately. Downgrades are not permitted.

What happens if a protocol doesn't succeed?

+

The Syndicate Ledger is diversified across multiple protocols. If one protocol underperforms, others may compensate. Your access rights remain valid, and you maintain subdomain/agent licenses even if revenue is lower than projected.

How do I access my dashboard and benefits?

+

Within 24 hours of payment, you'll receive an email with your dashboard login credentials, onboarding guide, and access to the Syndicate Ledger portal where you can monitor protocols, claim benefits, and track earnings.

Are there any ongoing fees or commitments?

+

No ongoing fees. Your one-time contribution grants you permanent access to your tier benefits. There are no monthly subscriptions, maintenance fees, or additional commitments required.

Can I transfer or sell my Syndicate position?

+

Syndicate positions are non-transferable and tied to your identity. However, subdomain assets and AI agent licenses you acquire may be transferable depending on the specific protocol terms.
